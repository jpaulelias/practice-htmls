    Current Interests:

    Funemployment can be daunting. Luckily, I've found ways to fill my days

    • Language learning

      • Over the last few months, I've gotten into language learning and discovered a community of polyglots whose advice I found really inspiring. All of them claim no special gift for language learning beyond being really motivated. Moreover, there's some consensus that if one were to spend an hour a day, every day for a year studying French, for example, in an effective way, by the year's end you'd be at about intermediate fluency.

      I've always wanted to be multilingual, so I started my own five-year plan. This year, I'll try to learn Turkish and Italian simultaneously since I have a a lot of free time. Next year, I'll give a year to Dutch, then French, then German all while maintaining as best I can the preceding languages. Three months into this five-year plan and it's going well!

    • Basketball

      • About every other day I go up to the park to shoot some hoops. I'm getting pretty good with my left-hand even, Hopefully this is also the year the Sacramento Kings stop being a national laughingstock. One can dream. Insallah as the Turks say.
